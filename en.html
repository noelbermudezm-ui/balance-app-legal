Privacy Policy for Balance App

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

Thank you for using Balance. Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

For the application to function, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and a password to protect your account.
  • Financial Information: Data on expenses, income, budgets, savings goals, debts, and loans that you enter yourself.
  • Photos (Optional): Receipt images that you choose to upload.
  • Usage and Diagnostic Data: Anonymous information about how you use the app and error reports to help us improve.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the application's features.
  • Securely sync your data so you can access it.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Analyze app performance to fix errors.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security and Retention

All information traveling between your device and our servers is encrypted (using HTTPS). We do our best to protect your data.

Receipt Images: To protect your privacy, all receipt images are automatically deleted from our systems 90 days after being uploaded.

4. Your Rights (Data Deletion)

You have full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and all your information from the "Settings" section within the application.

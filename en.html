Privacy Policy for Balance App

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use our Service.

By using the Service, You agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Definitions

  • Application means Balance, the software program provided by the Developer.
  • Account means a unique account created for You to access our Service.
  • Developer (referred to as either "We", "Us" or "Our") refers to Noel Bermudez, the individual developer of the Balance application.
  • Personal Data is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service refers to the Application.
  • You means the individual accessing or using the Service.

2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Contact Information: Email address and name.
  • Financial Information: Information you voluntarily provide about your expenses, income, budgets, savings goals, debts, and loan details.
  • Photos and Files: Receipt images that you choose to upload from your device's camera or photo library.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with the application, such as features used and crash reports.

Use of Your Personal Data

We use Your Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including saving and syncing your financial information.
  • To manage Your Account and give you access to the application's functionalities.
  • To contact You with important notifications about your account or the service.
  • To improve the application by analyzing anonymous usage data and fixing bugs.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We may share Your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We use third-party services like Firebase (Google) to host the database, manage authentication, and analyze performance. These providers are obligated to protect your information.
  • With other users (in your family group): If you use the family feature, information about expenses you designate as shared will be visible to other members of your private group. No information is ever shared publicly.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Retention of Your Personal Data

We will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently erased.

Image Policy: To protect your privacy, all receipt images are automatically deleted from our systems 90 days after they are uploaded.

Delete Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete Your Personal Data. You can delete your account and all associated information at any time from the "Settings" section of the application.

3. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18.
